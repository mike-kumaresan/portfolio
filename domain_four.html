    Ins-PI-re! [2022]

    I led and organized inaugural Ins-PI-re event on 3/14/22. Ins-PI-re is the Mathematics Department's annual celebration of Pi-Day in which we aim to engage students in mathematical enrichment that goes beyond our traditional curriculum. For our inaugural event, we offered eight lectures and workships ran by Department members for students during each period of the day. We also gave out free pie to students who attended the lecures and did a fundraiser in honor of Mr. Bruce Henry. I will be continuing the work for subsequent academic years with the hope of making the event a District-wide celebration of STEM.

    In the 2020-2021 academic year, I collaborated with Alan Killian to write some software to automate the game scheduling process for the Athletics Department. The software has been helpful to the Athletics department in reducing the amount of time it takes to propogate changes to relevant parties and inform them when the game schedule changes. Professional Development

    CSTA Virtual Conference [2019, 2020]

    During the summer of 2020, I attended the Computer Science Teachers' Association Annual Conference. The conference was held virtually due to the outbreak of the pandemic. One highlight of the conference was that I was able to connect with a representative from Phidgets, Inc. After some discussions and experimentation, I was able to persuade the STEAM department to use Phidget products. Phidgets are now an important part of the Introductory Engineering course curriculum.
